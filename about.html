ABOUT US

Irsbaccom University was founded in 2010 by Dr. Julien Chambi Atchade and is headquartered in Parakou, a city in the northern region of Benin republic. Over the years, the university has continued to be a meeting ground for students of different nationalities;Benin, Togo, Niger, Burkina Faso, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Mali and Nigeria. She has been recognized on various international levels for her outstanding performance in delivering qualitative education to her students and her relentless approach in achieving her goals and objectives. IRSBACCOM University is partnered with many foreign institutions around the globe and leaves her doors open to many more prospective partnerships.
